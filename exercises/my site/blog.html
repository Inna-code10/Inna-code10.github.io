Dyson

Dyson attachments vary by purpose: stylers (like the Airwrap) feature cylindrical attachments for creating curls, straightening brushes for smoothing hair, and a comb for curly hair. TV Samsung

Samsung TVs are typically Smart TVs with internet connectivity and advanced features, including access to apps and streaming services.
